Thank you for choosing Miraj Islamic School as a place for your child to learn and grow! Miraj Islamic School strives for learners who will develop to their fullest potential.

Visit our school or simply download an online application form for your child. The application form must be accompanied by:
  • Registration fee of $200 (non-refundable)
  • A copy of your child's birth certificate
  • Previous school's transcript/report card/all state wide exam scores
  • Up to date immunization record/medical record
  • Two recent photographs of your child

Upon submission of all documents, an admission test date will be scheduled.

*Subject to space availability.
