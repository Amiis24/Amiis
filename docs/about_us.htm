ABOUT US

 

MISSION

The mission of Miraj Islamic School is to provide excellent all-round education in a pleasant and stimulating Islamic environ- ment.  We strive for an excellent standard of education, and try our best to inculcate Islamic manners in our students to become exemplary citizens.  

PROGRAM

The school curriculum follows the State of New York guidelines. Miraj Islamic School curriculum provides young learners with solid educational foundation in the language arts, math, science, social studies, Islam and Qur’anic studies

MAP

FACILITY

Miraj Islamic School’s foundation is Islam, overall academics and discipline, with complete trust in Allah (s.w.t.).  The 37,000 sq ft facility gives our teachers and students the setting they need to engage in all types of indoor educational, physical and spiritual activities. The building contains fifteen spacious classrooms, a state of the art computer lab (with thirty computers), a library, a multi-purpose gymnasium, a commercial kitchen, a cafeteria, offices for staff and a big mosque. This complex provides stimulation for development and understanding. The pleasant environment is conducive for teaching and learning.

REMEMBER

The “Miraj”, the ascension from the earth to the heavens, is a significant event in the life of Prophet Muhammad (s.a.w.). He (s.a.w.) traveled from Makkah to Jerusalem, then from Jerusalem to the heavens and back home, all in one night. The Miraj Islamic School is dedicated to striving towards the goal of “Miraj”, by stressing Islam as the main focus of academia. This foundation will help insure the development of well-rounded individuals, dedicated to  understand and practice Islam at all times.
